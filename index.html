We're Alex and Zoe

We're a couple of passionate, dog loving, hard working, tree hugging recent college grads pursuing our next big adventure. In February 2018 we were three months from being thrown into the real world. With no plans in place, but plenty of enthusiasm and a fair share of anxiety, we considered our next step. We were feeling tired of the way we’ve been learning for the past 18 years - memorizing facts for exams with little room for creativity and exploration. Both of us dreamed of expanding our horizons and growing our breadth of experiences in new ways. Our adventurous spirits and love of nature made the choice easy: move into a mini-van and travel across the country in search of a new place to live, work, and grow! Armed with a Honda Odyssey (shout out to Alex’s parents), enough money for a few weeks of meals and gas, and a long list of places to visit, we have begun piecing together plans for our Mini-Van Life road trip.

Where we're headed

Starting in late July we'll be following the route to the right out west - check out the map, you can see each place we plan to be. We will meet up with friends in various cities and National Parks along our journey. We will visit parts of the country we’ve never been to before. We will expand our skills of creativity, photography, various outdoor sports, and being adaptable. Our end goal of this trip is to evaluate cities along the west coast and south west to decide where we would like to live and work. Find out how we plan to execute this big adventure.
