We're Alex and Zoe



We're a couple of passionate, dog-loving, hard-working, tree-hugging recent college grads pursuing our next big adventure. In February 2018 we were three months from being thrown into the real world. With no plans in place, but enough enthusiasm and thirst for adventure to compensate, we considered our next step. We were feeling tired of the way we’ve been learning for the past 18 years - memorizing facts for exams with little room for creativity and exploration. Both of us dreamed of expanding our horizons and growing our breadth of experiences in new ways. Our adventurous spirits and love of nature made the choice easy: move into a mini-van and travel across the country in search of a new place to live, work, and grow! Armed with a Honda Odyssey, enough money for a few weeks of meals and gas, and a long list of places to visit, we have begun piecing together plans for our Mini-Van Life road trip.

Mini-Van Adventure

Starting in late July we'll be heading west following the route to the right. We will meet up with friends in various cities and National Parks along our journey. Our trip allows us to visit parts of the country we’ve never been to before. Throwing ourselves into the unknown will strengthen our skills of adaptability, creativity, photography, and various outdoor sports. Our end goal of this trip is to evaluate cities along the west coast and south west to decide where we would like to live and work. Find out how we plan to execute this big adventure, check out our travel page.
