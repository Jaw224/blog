Interested in playing a role in our adventure?

We love to live frugally during our travels and in our day to day lives. While living in a van cuts out some costs, and allows us freedom to be able to travel, it comes with its own share of unavoidable expenses. While we are traveling we will be spending time documenting our journey and sharing our experiences, while looking for our new home. If you enjoy the content that this lifestyle produces and want to support our travels, we have created a couple ways for you to actively play a role in our adventure! You can purchase a “souvenir” of sorts that we will personally make and send to you, this will also directly support our adventure and pursuit of finding a new home. Below are some ways we believe we can share our experiences with you in a very personal and creative way!

IMPORTANT NOTE: If you make a contribution, please enter your email address under “Add special instructions to the seller:” in PayPal so we can make sure we have all your information! If you are contributing towards multiple souvenirs please mention which ones you are expecting.


$5

Thank you email!

We will send you an email with a photo of where we are and a message about our day.

$25

Postcards!

We will send you a postcard once a month for three months from our trip and include a message about our time in that place.

$50

Watercolor and Sketch!

We will send you a unique page from Alexs sketchbook and a watercolor study done by Zoe. We will accomapny this art with a postcard thanking you for your support!

$100

Hire us to Serve!

We are passionate about having an impact in the communities we are visiting. If you choose to support at this tier we will each complete five hours of community service in exchange for gas and food money. You will choose the area of focus (fighting hunger, environmental stewardship, education, politics, etc.) and we will research, complete service with, and blog about the program we choose to partner with. We will send you a postcard and polaroid thanking you for your support as well as mentioning you in our blog.
